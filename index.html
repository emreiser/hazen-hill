About us

We are a small, family & friend-run farm based in Lincoln, MA that focuses on cultivating and sharing beautiful cut flowers using organic, regenerative & sustainable practices.

We delight in reimagining the use of the suburban lawn to create diverse & productive growing spaces, foster community, and reclaim land to support native species & ecosystems.
